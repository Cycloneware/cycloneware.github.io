Grand Opening: Cycloneware is Live!

We are excited to announce the grand opening of Cycloneware! Our site is in its early stages of construction and is currently in beta. We’re working hard to add more features and fix bugs. Stay tuned for exciting updates as we continue to improve!

Looking to the Future: What’s Coming to Cycloneware

We have big plans for Cycloneware! In the future, we will introduce Cyclone Life, a life simulation game, and Cyclone Social, a new social media platform. Although we tried to develop these sections earlier, we ran into too many bugs and confusion. This time, we’re committed to not deleting our GitHub repository and pushing forward until everything is working smoothly.

Join Our Discord Server!

We are excited to announce that Cycloneware now has a Discord server! The server is still being set up, but you can join us and be part of the community as we continue development. Stay updated on the latest news and interact with other users. You can join here: Discord Invite

Social Cyclone is Under Construction!

We are excited to announce that Social Cyclone is now officially under construction. This new social platform will use Firebase and Google Cloud to manage account databases and ensure secure authentication. We’re working hard to bring the best experience to our users. More updates will be coming soon!

Firebase Issues on Social Cyclone

We've encountered some issues with Firebase on Social Cyclone that are currently preventing certain features from working as expected. We're aware of the problem and are working hard to resolve it. Our team is doing everything we can to get it fixed and will update you as soon as the issue is resolved. Thank you for your patience as we continue to improve Social Cyclone!
