What's Coming to CycloneWare: Future Features and Lessons Learned

December 26, 2024

At CycloneWare, we have big plans for the future. Some of the exciting features we are working on include:

  • Cyclone Life: A life simulation game where you can experience various life scenarios and create your own virtual life.
  • Cyclone Social: A social media platform to connect with other CycloneWare users, share updates, and socialize in a virtual world.
  • And much more!

While we are excited about these features, we have to be transparent about our past experiences. We initially tried to implement these sections but faced numerous issues like bugs, confusion, and technical challenges that delayed our progress. As a result, we ended up scrapping the features and deleting the repo on GitHub.

However, this time we're committed to finishing what we started. We have learned from our past mistakes, and we're determined not to repeat them. We will not be deleting the repository again. We believe in CycloneWare, and we are going to bring these ideas to life no matter what.

Stay tuned for updates as we continue to improve CycloneWare and bring these features to life in the near future. We appreciate your patience and support!
